Together We End Coronavirus

Wuhan is the largest metropolitan area in central China, with a population of 11 million. In December 2019, a new coronavirus was detected in Wuhan. The city was under a lockdown as an effort to contain the virus. Up to this point, the virus has affected thousands of Wuhan residents and caused hundreds of deaths.

Wuhan is in a state of emergency. Wuhan needs your help.

Wuhan United is working to support medical stuffs in Wuhan and provide coverage & awareness of the disease. If you wish to know more about the situation and our fundraising initiative, please check out What's Happening and Who We are.
